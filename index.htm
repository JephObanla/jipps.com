About the Journal

The Journal of IP Professionals (JIPPS) is a peer-reviewed publication dedicated to advancing insight into intellectual property (IP) for innovation and impact in the digital age.
At JIPPS, we bridge the gap between academia, policy, and practice by providing a dynamic platform where scholarly research, practitioner expertise, and policy-driven perspectives intersect. We are committed to fostering meaningful dialogue and delivering thought leadership that is both intellectually rigorous and practically relevant.
We publish original research, expert commentary, case studies, and professional insights that examine the evolving role of IP in our globally interconnected and technology-driven world. Our diverse readership and contributors span startups, creators, legal professionals, policymakers, and academics across jurisdictions.

Key Focus Areas :

  • Intellectual Property Law and Policy
  • IP in Emerging Technologies (AI, Blockchain, Digital Assets)
  • Innovation, Startups, and IP Strategy
  • Global IP Developments and Comparative Perspectives
  • IP and Public Interest
  • IP Protection for Emerging Economies

Whether you are a scholar, policy advocate, legal expert, or IP enthusiast, JIPPS is your intellectual home for timely, relevant, and impactful discussions shaping the future of IP.


Mission Statement : To advance high-quality scholarship and professional insight on intellectual property by publishing peer-reviewed research, fostering dialogue among academics, practitioners, and policymakers, and supporting informed approaches to innovation and regulation in a globalized economy.


Vision Statement : To be a globally recognized journal at the forefront of intellectual property thought leadership, driving innovation, influencing policy, and shaping practical solutions for the evolving IP landscape.
